Meat:
-1 lb of Pork tenderloin

Produce:
-1 Cilantro
-1 Jalapeno
-1 Lime
-1 crunchy Onion
-1 cup of fresh Pineapple chunks

Canned Goods:
-1 14-ounce can of full fat Coconut milk

Condiments:
-1/4 cup of Teriyaki sauce

Pasta & Grains:
-2 cups of Jasmine rice

Oils & Vinegars:
-1 tbsp of Olive oil

Liquids:
-1 and 1/2 cups of Water
